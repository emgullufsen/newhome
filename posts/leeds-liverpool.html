📚 Now to books…

In Civil War Vol. 3 the Union forces have really turned the corner and the Confederacy is in full ebb-tide. Their soldiers are in tatters – their officer corps shot up. I'm enjoying reading about the Union forces charging through Georgia on Sherman's "march to the sea" to Savannah on the seaboard. Grant and Sherman really had a special friendship and supported each other in way that's actually quite touching. Here is a quote Foote has from Sherman

Grant is a great general. I know him well. He stood by me when I was crazy and I stood by him when he was drunk. And now, sir, we stand by each other always.
- W.T. Sherman, excerpted from The Civil War: A Narrative, Volume Three: Red River to Appomattox
